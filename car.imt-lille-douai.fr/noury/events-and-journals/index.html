Events and Journals I am involved In

I am a member of the Technical Committee on Software Engineering for Robotics and Automation (TC-Soft) of the IEEE Robotics and Automation Society (RAS) since 2013. I’ve been board member of the European Smalltalk Users Group (ESUG) from 2003 to 2010. As such, I have participated every year to the organization of the ESUG International Smalltalk Joint Conference. I have also been the founder (in 2004) and the chair of the ESUG Tech Awards till 2010. Besides, I have also been contributing to the following events and journal as a program/editorial committee or reviewer.
